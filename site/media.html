  • Portland mayoral candidates compete to praise bicycling, walking and transit

    Jeff Mapes of the Oregonian covered PSU Planning Club’s mayoral forum event Local Motion: Portland Mayoral Candidates Discuss Active Transportation.

    We were there too. We organized a pre-party at Hotlips Pizza, an after-party at Rogue, and we lead the twitter coverage (#PDXMayor).

    On Monday night I was at one of the countless number of forums featuring the three major candidates for Portland mayor. This event – sponsored by nine groups supportive of cycling, walking and transit – packed the main auditorium in Portland State University’s 475-seat Lincoln Hall and was a good example of how interest groups can use these kinds of forums to prod candidates in their direction.
